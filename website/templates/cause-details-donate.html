The Education Fund helps disadvantaged young people around the world to access education. To assist these young people on their path to education, personal growth, and social involvement, we provide support to hand-picked promising educational projects in areas where funding is either difficult to obtain or non-existent. The Education Fund has donated to educational projects across the globe, from Kashmir to KanyaKumari and beyond. To find out more about specific projects and what you can do to get involved. We are open to homeless charities, poverty prevention schemes and housing associations. Your donation is used for prevention of homelessness, care of the homeless .
